# ReMynd Assessment Operating System (RAOS)
## Invigilator Training Manual

---

## 1. Your Role

As an **Assessment Invigilator**, you are responsible for conducting virtual assessment sessions with students. You facilitate the live administration of assessment tools, manage the virtual meeting room, and ensure the session runs smoothly.

Your access in RAOS is **view-only for case management** — you cannot advance phases or modify case data — but you have full access to the tools you need to run assessment sessions.

---

## 2. Logging In

1. Go to the RAOS system URL provided by your administrator.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. You will land on the **Dashboard**.

> If you have forgotten your password, contact your administrator.

---

## 3. Your Navigation Menu

After logging in, you will see the following menu items in the left sidebar:

| Menu Item | What it does |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of active cases and recent activity |
| **Cases** | List of all active cases — click any case to open it |
| **Inquiries** | New referral inquiries submitted through the parent portal |

> You will **not** see Assessment Tools or Team Management — those are admin-only areas.

---

## 4. The Case Lifecycle

Cases move through the following phases. Your active involvement is primarily in the **Assessment** phase:

```
Pre-Commitment → Intake → Setup → Forms → Assessment → Scoring → Report → Final Review → Debrief → Complete
```

When a case is created, you are **automatically assigned** to it as the assessment invigilator and will receive a notification email with a direct link to the case.

---

## 5. Opening a Case

1. Click **Cases** in the sidebar.
2. Find the student's case (cases are sorted by most recently updated).
3. Click the row to open the case detail page.

The top of the case page shows:
- The **phase stepper** (which step the case is currently on)
- **Student Details** — name, school, grade, language, parent contact
- The current **phase panel** relevant to your work

---

## 6. The Assessment Phase — Your Primary Responsibility

When a case reaches the **Assessment** phase, your job is to:

### Step 1 — Set Up the Virtual Meeting Room

A green **Invigilation Meeting Room** card appears on the case page during the Assessment phase.

1. Click **Open Jitsi Moderated Meetings ↗** — this opens the Jitsi website in a new tab.
2. On the Jitsi page, a moderated room will be created. Copy the **"Share meeting link for guests"** URL shown on that page.
3. Return to RAOS and paste the URL into the **Step 2 — Paste the Guest Link** field.
4. Click **Save Guest Link**.

The guest link is now saved. You will use this to invite the student/parent to join.

> **Important:** The Moderator link (shown on the Jitsi site) is for you — the invigilator. The Guest link is for the student. Never share your Moderator link.

### Step 2 — Share the Meeting Link with the Student

Once the guest link is saved:
- A **Copy Guest Link** button appears — use this to copy the link and share it directly with the student or parent via your preferred communication channel (email, message, etc.).
- A **Join as Moderator** button lets you enter the meeting room as the session host.

### Step 3 — Conduct the Session

During the session:
- The student joins via the guest link; you join as moderator.
- Work through the assigned assessment tools with the student.
- The tools assigned to you as "Invigilator" appear in the **Assessment Forms & Assignments** section of the case.

### Step 4 — Complete Your Assessment Forms

After or during the session:
1. In the **Assessment Forms & Assignments** section, find the tools assigned to respondent type **Invigilator**.
2. These forms track observations made during the live session.
3. Mark them complete when done.

---

## 7. Scoring Phase — View Only

When the case moves to **Scoring**, you can view the scores panel but **cannot recalculate scores**. Score calculations are performed by the psychometrician or administrator.

You can review:
- Domain scores per tool
- Respondent breakdowns
- Any high discrepancy flags

---

## 8. Report Phase — View Only

During the Report and Final Review phases, you can view the **Report Access** panel to monitor:
- Whether the report has been uploaded
- Which recipients have received their links
- Whether parents have downloaded the report

You cannot upload documents or send emails in this section — that is handled by the administrator.

---

## 9. Inquiries

If you receive a new referral through the parent inquiry portal, it will appear in your **Inquiries** section with a badge showing the count of new items.

1. Click **Inquiries** in the sidebar.
2. Review the incoming referral details.
3. Contact your administrator to discuss next steps — you cannot create a new case yourself.

---

## 10. What You Cannot Do

To protect case integrity, your account has the following restrictions:

| Action | Available to you? |
|--------|------------------|
| Advance or step back phases | No |
| Create a new case | No |
| Edit student details | No |
| Upload reports or send report emails | No |
| Manage team members | No |
| View Assessment Tools library | No |
| Recalculate scores | No |

---

## 11. Tips for a Smooth Assessment Session

- **Test the meeting room before the session** — open a Jitsi room at least 10 minutes early and verify your audio/video.
- **Share the guest link in advance** — send it to the parent or student at least 24 hours before the session so they can test their connection.
- **Use headphones** — reduces echo and improves audio quality for the student.
- **Record your observations immediately** — complete your invigilator forms as soon as the session ends while details are fresh.
- **Contact your administrator** if the case is not yet in the Assessment phase — you cannot set up the meeting room until the phase is active.

---

## 12. Getting Help

If you encounter any issues:

- **Login problems** → Contact your administrator to reset your password.
- **Case not visible** → You may not have been assigned yet; contact your administrator.
- **Meeting room issues** → Try refreshing Jitsi or creating a new room and updating the guest link in RAOS.
- **Any other system issue** → Contact your ReMynd system administrator.
