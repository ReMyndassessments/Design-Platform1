# ReMynd Assessment Operating System (RAOS)
## Invigilator Training Manual

---

## 1. Your Role

As an Assessment Invigilator, you are responsible for conducting virtual assessment sessions with students. You facilitate the live administration of assessment tools, join the virtual meeting room, and ensure the session runs smoothly.

You also play an active role in the early stages of client engagement — you can send referral form invitations directly to schools or contacts, and host ad-hoc client meetings using Quick Meet.

Your access in RAOS for case management is view-only — you cannot advance phases, modify case data, or configure the invigilation meeting room for a specific case. The administrator handles those steps.

---

## 2. Logging In

1. Go to the RAOS system URL provided by your administrator.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. You will land on the Dashboard.
4. If you have forgotten your password, contact your administrator.

---

## 3. Your Navigation Menu

| Menu Item | What it does |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | Overview of active cases and recent activity |
| Cases | List of all active cases — click any case to open it |
| Inquiries | New referral inquiries and the Send Referral Form tool |
| Quick Meet | Generate an instant branded meeting room and share the link |

You will not see Assessment Tools or Team Management — those are admin-only areas.

---

## 4. The Case Lifecycle

Cases move through the following phases. Your active involvement is primarily in the Pre-Commitment, Intake, Assessment, and Debrief phases:

**Pre-Commitment → Intake → Setup → Forms → Assessment → Scoring → Report → Final Review → Debrief → Complete**

When a case is created, you are automatically assigned to it as the assessment invigilator and will receive a notification email with a direct link to the case.

---

## 5. Opening a Case

1. Click **Cases** in the sidebar.
2. Find the student's case (cases are sorted by most recently updated).
3. Click the row to open the case detail page.

The top of the case page shows:

- The phase stepper (which step the case is currently on)
- Student Details — name, school, grade, language, parent contact
- During the Assessment phase: a full-width Invigilation Meeting Room panel at the top of the page, followed by your assigned assessment forms below

---

## 6. The Assessment Phase — Your Primary Responsibility

During the Assessment phase, your case page is arranged in two stacked sections:

1. **Invigilation Meeting Room panel** — appears at the top, spanning the full-page width
2. **Assessment Forms & Assignments** — listed below the meeting room panel

*Note: In some cases where the assessment is face-to-face without admin or clinical collaboration, the meeting room may not be necessary.*

### Step 1 — Join the Meeting Room

The administrator sets up the virtual meeting room and sends the joining link to you (and to the student/parent) by email before the session.

When you open the case during the Assessment phase, the green Invigilation Meeting Room panel appears at the top of the page:

- **If the room is configured:** you will see the Assessment Date & Time and a green "Join Assessment Meeting ↗" button.
- **If not yet configured:** the panel will say "No meeting room has been set up yet. The admin will configure this and notify you."

Click **Join Assessment Meeting ↗** to enter the session. The meeting opens in a new browser tab.

**Important:** You do not need to create or configure the case meeting room — this is handled entirely by your administrator. If the join button has not appeared before the session, contact your administrator immediately.

### Step 2 — Conduct the Session

During the session:

- The student/parent joins via the guest link sent to them by the administrator.
- You join as the session moderator using the "Join Assessment Meeting ↗" button in RAOS.
- Work through the assigned assessment tools with the student.
- The tools assigned to you as "Invigilator" are listed in the Assessment Forms & Assignments section below the meeting room panel.

### Step 3 — Complete Your Assessment Forms

After or during the session:

- In the Assessment Forms & Assignments section, find the tools assigned to respondent type **Invigilator** (Assessment Observation Form).
- These forms track observations made during the live session.
- Complete and submit each form when done.

---

## 7. Scoring Phase — View Only

When the case moves to Scoring, you can view the scores panel but cannot recalculate scores. Score calculations are performed by the psychometrician or administrator.

You can review:
- Domain scores per tool
- Respondent breakdowns
- Any high discrepancy flags

---

## 8. Report Phase — View Only

During the Report and Final Review phases, you can view the Report Access panel to monitor:

- Whether the report has been uploaded
- Which recipients have received their links
- Whether parents have downloaded the report

You cannot upload documents or send emails in this section — that is handled by the administrator.

## 9. Inquiries — Receiving and Sending Referrals

Click **Inquiries** in the sidebar to access two things: incoming referral submissions from the parent/school portal, and the Send Referral Form tool.

### 9a. Reviewing Incoming Referrals

The Inquiries list shows all referral forms submitted by schools or parents through the external portal. Each entry shows the contact name, school, enquiry type, and status. You can update the status of an enquiry (e.g. mark it as Contacted or Converted) and leave it for the administrator to open a full case.

### 9b. Sending a Referral Form Invitation

You can send a referral form link directly to a school contact or parent without waiting for the administrator. This is useful when you have spoken with a school and want to follow up immediately.

**To send a referral invitation:**

1. Click **+ Send Referral Form** at the top of the Inquiries page. A panel slides open.
2. **Choose the form type** by clicking the appropriate pill:
   - Referral — School *(default)*
   - Referral — Corporate
   - Referral — University
   - Referral — Parent
   - Referral — Boarding
3. **Optionally tick "Also include Consent Form"** — this adds the Consent Form to the same link. The contact completes both forms in a single visit; you do not need to send a separate link.
4. **Fill in the contact details:**
   - Contact Name *(required)*
   - Email Address *(required)*
   - School Name *(optional)*
   - Personal note *(optional — this appears in the body of the email)*
5. **Choose how to send:**
   - **Send Email Invite** — RAOS sends a branded email to the contact with a secure portal link. Use this for formal email outreach.
   - **Copy Link Only** — Copies the secure portal link to your clipboard silently, with no email sent. Use this when you want to share the link yourself via WhatsApp, WeChat, or another channel.

**Important — no case is created yet.** Sending the invitation (by email or by copying the link) does not create a case in the system. The case is only created automatically once the contact actually opens the link and submits the referral form. You can send the same link to the same person via multiple channels (email and WhatsApp, for example) without creating duplicate cases — the link is a single token tied to a single invite.

Once the contact submits the form, a new case will appear in the Cases list and the administrator will be notified to review it and advance it to the next phase.

## 10. Quick Meet — Instant Branded Meeting Rooms

The **Quick Meet** button appears at the bottom of your sidebar. Use it to start an ad-hoc branded meeting with a parent, school contact, or colleague — for example, for a pre-assessment consultation or an informal check-in call — without needing the administrator to set anything up.

**How to use Quick Meet:**

1. Click **Quick Meet** in the sidebar. A dialog opens.
2. A room name is generated automatically (e.g. *remynd-coral-summit-412*). You can edit it or click the refresh icon to generate a new one.
3. The **Guest link** is shown — this is the link you share with the other person. They will land on a branded ReMynd join page. No account, no app download required.
4. Click **Copy** to copy the guest link, then paste it into your message (email, WhatsApp, etc.).
5. Click **Join as Host** to open the meeting room in a new tab. Join at least a few minutes before your guest so you are ready when they arrive.

**Note:** Quick Meet rooms are not stored in the system — there is no record of the meeting in RAOS. Use this for informal meetings only. Formal assessment sessions are set up by the administrator through the case meeting room and are tied to a specific case.

| Recalculate scores | No |

## 12. Tips for a Smooth Assessment Session

- **Join the meeting room early** — click "Join Assessment Meeting" at least 10 minutes before the session to verify your audio and video.
- **Confirm the meeting link is ready** — check RAOS before the session. If the green join button has not appeared, contact your administrator.
- **Use headphones** — reduces echo and improves audio quality for the student.
- **Record your observations immediately** — complete your invigilator forms as soon as the session ends while details are fresh.
- **Contact your administrator if the case is not yet in the Assessment phase** — the meeting room panel only appears once the case reaches this phase.
- **For Quick Meet calls, join a few minutes early** — room links are instant but giving yourself a moment to check audio before the guest arrives keeps things professional.
- **Send referral links via the channel the contact prefers** — if a school coordinator is more responsive on WhatsApp, use Copy Link Only and send it there. If they prefer email, use Send Email Invite. Both create the same secure link.

## 13. Getting Help

| Issue | What to do |
|---|---|
| Login problems | Contact your administrator to reset your password |
| Case not visible | You may not have been assigned yet — contact your administrator |
| "Join Assessment Meeting" button not visible | The administrator has not configured the meeting room yet — contact your administrator |
| Meeting room connection issues | Contact your administrator to update the meeting link in RAOS |
| Referral invite not received by contact | Check the email address entered; resend using Copy Link Only and share manually |
| Any other system issue | Contact your ReMynd system administrator |
